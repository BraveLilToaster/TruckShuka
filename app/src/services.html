Consultation

Before we do any measurements, we want to get to know what your wardrobe needs are. Once we have a comprehensive understanding of what those needs are, we move into our detailed measurement methods to get the perfect fit to your physique. We continue to keep records of your individual style, fabric, color preferences, and construction profiles for all garments that we create for you.

Bespoke / Bench-Grade Tailoring

Our master tailors take your exact measurements and specifications to create each garment from scratch in our stately tailor room. We take 26 precise individual measurements to create a suit that fits you and no one else. Our endless fabric selection accompanied by our unlimited color and pattern schemes make any suit possible. We also have master tailors equipped for any style suit single breasted, two button, three button, European, the list is endless. We take suit making seriously and so should you.

Custom Shirts

To enhance your wardrobe in any way that we can, we realize that custom is king. So, using thirteen precise measurements we develop a custom shirt that is designed to your physique and fit. Much like our suits we offer an array of fabrics, color and pattern designs that will enhance any shirt for any occasion.

Formal Wear

No matter what event it is, we guarantee we can create the perfect garment. Our master tailor’s custom-design dinner jackets, formal shirts, dress trousers, gowns and tuxedos for the most prestigious of events. At Cicchini Custom Clothier we create an exciting process where you will select the exact style you envision and we make it a reality, down to the very last detail.
